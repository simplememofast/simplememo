Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Simple Memo is designed to be privacy-friendly. We believe your data is yours alone. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Data You Provide

The only data you provide to Simple Memo is:

  • The memo content you type in the app
  • The recipient email address when you choose to send a memo

Where Your Notes Are Stored

Notes are stored locally on your device only. We do not store, access, or back up your notes on our servers. Your memos remain entirely under your control.

Email Delivery (Resend)

When you choose to send a memo by email, the memo content and recipient email address are sent to Resend solely to deliver the email. Resend may process this data as needed for delivery and does not retain it beyond what is necessary for processing.

Analytics / Tracking / Ads

Simple Memo does not collect analytics data, track your activity, or display advertisements. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or third parties.

Account / Login

No account is required to use Simple Memo. The app works entirely on your device without any login or registration.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@simplememofast.com
