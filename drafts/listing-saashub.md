## Full Description

SimpleMemo is an iPhone app that lets you email yourself a note in under a second. It launches in 0.3 seconds, presents a clean text input, and sends your memo to your own inbox with one tap.

Designed for people who capture ideas by emailing themselves, SimpleMemo eliminates the friction of opening a mail app, filling in the To field, adding a subject, and hitting send. Just open, type, send.

Built as a replacement for Captio (discontinued), SimpleMemo adds AES-GCM encryption for all messages, full offline support with automatic queuing, and a modern interface — while keeping the speed and simplicity that made the memo-to-email workflow popular.

Works with Gmail, Outlook, iCloud Mail, and any other email provider. No account creation beyond your email setup. No ads in any tier.
