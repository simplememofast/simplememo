## Full Description

SimpleMemo is an iPhone app designed for a single workflow: capturing a thought and emailing it to yourself, as fast as possible.

The app launches in 0.3 seconds — independently benchmarked — and presents a clean, distraction-free text input. Type your note, tap send, and it arrives in your inbox. No folders, no tags, no organizational overhead. Your email inbox is your capture system.

SimpleMemo was built as a modern replacement for Captio, the popular memo-to-email app that was discontinued. It preserves the speed and simplicity of that workflow while adding enterprise-grade AES-GCM encryption, full offline support with automatic queuing, and a contemporary interface.

Ideal for GTD practitioners, busy professionals, product managers, and anyone who processes their ideas through email.
