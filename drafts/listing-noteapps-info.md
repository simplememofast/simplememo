## Long Description

SimpleMemo is an iPhone app purpose-built for one thing: capturing thoughts and sending them to your email inbox as fast as possible. The app launches in 0.3 seconds — independently benchmarked — so you can get your idea down before it disappears.

Unlike general-purpose note apps, SimpleMemo is designed around the "memo to self" workflow. Open the app, type your note, hit send, and it arrives in your inbox. No folders, no tags, no organization overhead. Your email inbox becomes your capture tool.

SimpleMemo was built as a replacement for Captio, the beloved memo-to-email app that was discontinued. It preserves the simplicity of that workflow while adding modern security (AES-GCM encryption for all messages), full offline support (notes queue and send automatically when connectivity returns), and a clean, distraction-free interface.

Perfect for GTD practitioners, busy professionals, and anyone who processes ideas through their email inbox.
