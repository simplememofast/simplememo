# SimpleMemo — Captio Alternative

## Short Description

SimpleMemo is a fast, privacy-focused memo-to-email app for iPhone. Write a note, tap send, and it arrives in your email inbox. Spiritual successor to Captio.

## Long Description

SimpleMemo is the fastest way to send a note to your own email inbox. Built as a direct successor to Captio, it focuses on one thing: getting your thoughts into your inbox before you forget them.

The app launches in 0.3 seconds thanks to a native UIKit implementation — no SwiftUI overhead. Notes are encrypted with AES-GCM before transmission, and nothing is stored on any server. Your data stays yours.

SimpleMemo works offline with an automatic outbox queue. Memos are sent as soon as connectivity returns, so you never lose a note — even underground.

No account creation required. Just set your email address and start sending. Free tier allows 3 memos per day. Premium (500 yen/month) unlocks unlimited sending.
