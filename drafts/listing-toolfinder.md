## One-Line Description

The fastest way to email yourself a note on iPhone — 0.3s launch, encrypted, offline-ready.

## Long Description

SimpleMemo is an iPhone app that does one thing perfectly: it lets you capture a thought and send it to your email inbox in under a second.

Launch the app in 0.3 seconds (independently benchmarked), type your note, tap send. That's it. No folders, no tags, no organization. Your email inbox is your capture tool.

Built as a modern replacement for Captio — the beloved memo-to-email app that was discontinued — SimpleMemo brings the same lightning-fast self-email workflow with added security and reliability:

- AES-GCM encryption protects every message
- Full offline support queues notes and sends them automatically when you're back online
- Works with any email provider: Gmail, Outlook, iCloud, Yahoo, and more

SimpleMemo is for people who think in email. GTD practitioners, product managers, founders, writers — anyone who captures ideas fastest by sending them to their own inbox.

Free plan includes 3 sends per day. Premium unlocks unlimited sends for $2.99/month or $29.99/year. No ads in any tier.
