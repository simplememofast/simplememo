Data & Privacy

Notes Stored Only on Your Device

All memos are stored exclusively on your device. We never store, access, or back up your notes on any server.

Email Delivery via Resend

When you send a memo via email, the note content and recipient email are sent to Resend solely to deliver the email.

No Analytics, No Tracking, No Ads

Simple Memo does not collect analytics data, track your activity, or display advertisements. We don't sell your data.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account?

No. Simple Memo works without any account. Just download, open, and start writing.

Where are my notes stored?

Your notes are stored only on your device. They never leave your iPhone unless you choose to email them.

What data is sent when I email a memo?

Only the memo content and recipient email address are sent to Resend for email delivery. No other data is transmitted, and Resend does not retain this information after delivery.
