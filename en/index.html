Our Story Why We Built This

We were long-time Captio users. The ability to send a thought to your inbox with one tap — that simple experience was unlike any other memo app. But in 2025, Captio shut down. We searched for an alternative with the same speed and simplicity, but nothing came close.

"If it doesn't exist, we'll build it ourselves." That decision launched this project. We achieved 0.3s launch and 150ms send speeds, built an Outbox architecture for reliable offline delivery, and designed a privacy-first system that never stores your memos on any server. Carrying forward Captio's spirit with modern technology, we created the best "note-to-email" experience possible.

Support FAQ

What is Simple Memo?

Simple Memo is a memo app focused on one thing: launch, write, send. Your memo is sent as an email to a pre-configured address with a single tap. There are no folders, tags, sync settings, or complex features.

Can I send memos while offline?

Yes. When you send a memo offline, it's stored in the Outbox — encrypted with AES-GCM on your device. When connectivity is restored, all pending memos are sent automatically.

Can I use Simple Memo for free?

Yes. The first 7 days are a free trial with full access to all features and unlimited sending. After the trial, you can continue on the free plan (3 memos per day).
