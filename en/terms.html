1. The Service

The Service is an iOS app that lets you write memos and email them to a recipient address you set. It also supports offline memo creation and "outbox" resend behavior when connectivity returns.

2. Features (No Functional Differences Between Plans)

Core features are the same on Free and Premium, including: creating memos, sending, history, pending/outbox management and resend, offline creation with online resend, language settings, recipient email setup and verification, subject/signature settings, and send sound.
The main difference between plans is the daily sending limit (Section 3).

3. Free Trial, Sending Limits, and Premium

  • Days 1 through 7: free trial period (unlimited sending)
  • From Day 8: Free plan is generally limited to 3 sends per day
  • Premium: disables the Free "3 sends/day" limit after Day 8, making sending effectively unlimited

Note: safety limits described in Section 4 may still apply.

4. Safety and Infrastructure Limits (All Plans)
