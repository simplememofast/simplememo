What Is the "Email Yourself" Note App Category

The "email yourself" note app category lets you write a memo on your phone and instantly send it to a specified email address. Both Note To Self Mail and Simple Memo belong to this category, leveraging email's permanence, searchability, and cross-device access. The core concept is shared, but they diverge significantly in launch speed, offline architecture, encryption implementation, and sending infrastructure — differences that compound into a noticeably different daily experience. Simple Memo's technical differentiators are: 0.3s launch, Outbox offline support, AES-GCM on-device encryption, and Cloudflare Workers + Resend API infrastructure.

3 Differences in Self-Email Methods

DIFFERENCE 01
One-Tap Send vs Recipient Entry
Note To Self Mail may require confirming or setting the recipient each time, but Simple Memo only needs your email set once — after that, every send is a single tap. Zero recipient entry friction means you stay focused on capturing the thought itself.
DIFFERENCE 02
Automatic Subject and Body Handling
Simple Memo automatically uses the first line of your memo as the email subject — no separate subject entry needed. Note To Self Mail may require entering subject and body separately. This automation further reduces the steps between thought and send.
DIFFERENCE 03
Send History Management
Simple Memo lets you review sent memo history within the app and manage unsent Outbox memos in a list view. Note To Self Mail's history features may be limited. Being able to look back at past memos within the app increases the utility of your note-taking habit.

Building a lasting self-email memo habit requires a few practical tips. First, place Simple Memo - for Obsidian in your home screen dock (the fixed area at the bottom). FAQ

Which app has fewer steps to send — Note To Self Mail or Simple Memo?

Simple Memo has fewer steps. After initial setup, the workflow is just two steps: launch the app (keyboard appears in 0.3s), type your memo, and tap send. No recipient entry or subject line setup required.

Does Simple Memo auto-generate email subjects?

Yes. Simple Memo automatically uses the first line of your memo as the email subject. No separate subject field to fill in, which further reduces the steps between thought and send.

Can I view my sent memo history within the app?

Yes. Simple Memo provides an in-app view of sent memos and lets you manage unsent Outbox memos. You can review past memos from within the app as well as your email inbox.

What happens to my old memos if I switch from Note To Self Mail?

All memos previously sent via Note To Self Mail remain in your email inbox. Simple Memo can use the same email address, so your old and new memos are unified in one inbox.
