For each P2:
- [ ] Open URL, verify Captio is actually mentioned (some are uncertain)
- [ ] Check if comments/contact still functional (forms, email, etc.)
- [ ] Customize template's `<NAME>`, `<URL_SLUG>`, `<utm_content>`

## Risk assessment per category

- **Blog comments / Medium responses (P2-03, P2-08, P2-09, P2-12, P2-14)**: Low risk; comment culture is welcoming
- **Email/contact form (P2-04, P2-06, P2-07, P2-10, P2-15-19, P2-22-24)**: Medium risk (spam filter); use TEMPLATE form
- **Twitter DM (P2-05)**: Medium-low risk; 1-DM-per-person max
- **Forums (P2-25, P2-26, P2-28, P2-29, P2-30)**: Medium risk (mods may consider promo); always lead with broken-link/historical-update angle
